Anime/Manga Library

This application is inspired by my interest in Anime and Manga. Due to the immense selection of Anime and Manga out there, I struggled to keep track of bookmark information such as episode or page number. So, I decided to fix the problem by using the skills I have aquired in my studies. The application allows users to record episode number, page number, rating, and any notes they may have regarding a particular piece of Anime or Manga. Other cool features include window resizing, image customization, and more.

    Languages/Tools Used

  • Java
  • JavaFX

School Portal

This project is currently in progress. The idea of the project is to create a nice way for students and teachers to communicate during the school year. I was inspired by the lack of real time access to information regarding grades and feedback during my high school years. Everything was paper based and slow. As I continue to develop this application, I hope this project will inspire high schools to adopt a more electronic approach regarding how students and teachers communicate.
